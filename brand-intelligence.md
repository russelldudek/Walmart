## Typography evidence and decision
Walmart states that the refreshed wordmark uses a custom font inspired by Sam Walton's classic trucker hat. No license for that proprietary custom face was identified for third-party use. The campaign uses Noto Sans, a licensed open-source humanist sans, and recreates the public brand character through large confident headings, compact labels, strong weights, and clear blue/yellow hierarchy. No proprietary font files are copied.

## Photography, geometry, and motion
The campaign avoids generic stock photography. It uses Walmart's refreshed high-contrast blue/yellow identity, strong rectangular geometry, product-label clarity, scan behavior, and simple directional movement. The role-derived visual metaphor is an original decision barcode: seven governance conditions are scanned into Fund, Shape, Hold, or Stop.
